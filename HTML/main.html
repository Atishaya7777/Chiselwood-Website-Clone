- ABOUT CHISELWOOD -

Hand made in Lincoln since 1989

When Mel and Martin Holliday founded Chiselwood in 1989 it was the principles of craftsmanship, quality and authenticity that fuelled their joint passion to live and work in Lincoln and create exquisitely crafted kitchens and furniture that they personally would want to live with, celebrating traditional British techniques from their own workshop.
